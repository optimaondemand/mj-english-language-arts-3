Lesson 2.16
When an Action Becomes a Thing
Grammar Lesson
📖 TODAY'S FOCUS
An -ing word can stop being a verb and start acting like a noun.
A gerund is a verb form that ends in -ing but works as a noun. In the sentence Exploring is dangerous, the word exploring names a thing, the activity itself, and it is the subject of the sentence. A gerund phrase is the gerund plus the words that go with it: Exploring an unknown planet takes courage. The whole phrase still acts as one noun. Gerund phrases let you turn an action into something you can talk about, which is useful when Ransom must describe what the creatures of Malacandra spend their lives doing.
🎯 BY THE END OF THIS LESSON YOU WILL BE ABLE TO…
1
I will be able to recognize a gerund acting as a noun in a sentence.
2
I will be able to tell a gerund phrase from an -ing verb that is part of the action.
📋 WHAT YOU NEED FOR THIS LESSON
▶
Your writing notebook and a pencil. About 5 to 10 minutes of focused practice.
📚 PART 1 · A WORKED EXAMPLE
⚡ QUICK CHECK-IN
In which sentence does the -ing word feel like a thing you could name, rather than something happening right now? (No wrong answer.)

Here is the test: if you can replace the -ing phrase with the word something or it and the sentence still works, the phrase is acting as a noun. That makes it a gerund phrase.

“Hunting the hnakra is the hrossa’s greatest test.” → “Hunting the hnakra” names an activity and is the subject. Gerund phrase.
“Ransom feared making a mistake before the seroni.” → “making a mistake” is the thing Ransom feared. Gerund phrase (object).
“Ransom was learning the language slowly.” → here “learning” is part of the action, what Ransom was doing. Not a gerund.
⚡ PART 2 · YOUR TURN

In each sentence, is the bold -ing phrase acting as a noun (a gerund phrase), or is it part of the verb?

▶  In your notebook, write one sentence that opens with a gerund phrase as its subject. Then click to see an example.
“Trusting a stranger from another world takes courage.” The phrase “Trusting a stranger from another world” is the subject and names an activity. Your own sentence will differ; check that your opening phrase names something and can be replaced with the word “it.”
💪 BEFORE YOU GO

A gerund turns an action into a noun you can talk about. Watch for -ing words this week and ask whether each one is happening or being named. Memory work: begin learning Drill passage 1.
