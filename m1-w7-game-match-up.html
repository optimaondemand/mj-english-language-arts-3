🎯 HOW TO PLAY
The left column holds verb phrases and subjects. The right column holds tense labels and matching verbs. Click one item on the left, then click the item on the right you think goes with it. A correct pair locks in green and fades out of play. A wrong pair flashes red and both stay available so you can try a new combination. Match all twelve pairs.

💪 WHY THIS HELPS

Matching a verb phrase to its tense, and a subject to the verb that agrees with it, trains the same instinct: find the true subject, and ask what job the verb is doing in time. You will use both skills every time you write about the novel.
